Privacy Policy

Perfect Tap respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Perfect Tap does not collect personal information directly.

The app may use third-party services such as Apple Game Center, Apple In-App Purchases, and Google AdMob. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Advertising

Perfect Tap uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to provide relevant ads. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple. Perfect Tap does not store or access your payment information.

Game Center

If you use Apple Game Center, Apple may collect gameplay and leaderboard data according to Apple’s privacy practices.

Data Storage

Game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to external servers by Perfect Tap.

Children’s Privacy

Perfect Tap is not directed toward children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
